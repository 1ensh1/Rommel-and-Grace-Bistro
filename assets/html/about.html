About Us

Rommel and Grace Bistro opened its doors in 2016 with a simple dream shared by Rommel and Grace. Originally starting with humble beginnings, they offered a variety of snacks including burgers and fries. Visiting Rommel's hometown, Lemery Batangas, changed the direction of their goals. They were introduced to Batangas Lomi. The rich flavors and warm reception sparked a new direction for their menu. With the support of their two sons, they focused their passion on perfecting their specialty Lomi, blending tradition with a commitment to quality and genuine hospitality.

Join us and taste the heart of Batangas at Rommel and Grace Bistro.
